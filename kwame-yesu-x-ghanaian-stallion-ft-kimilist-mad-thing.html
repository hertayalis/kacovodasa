Kwame Yesu X Ghanaian Stallion ft Kimilist Mad Thing

August 22, 2024
blog

Ghanaian rapper, Kwame Yesu teams up with music producer, Ghanaian Stallion to serve fans and music consumers with a brand new well curated single labeled; “Mad Thing”.

The single features young outstanding Ghanaian rapper, Kimilist. “Mad Thing” was taken off their joint body of work captioned; “Brought The Rage” Extended Play.

The latest EP houses six solid and dope tracks with contributions from talented Ghanaian recording artists. The entire project owes production credit to Ghanaian Stallion.

Kwame Yesu has been in the music scene for a while and has been consistent throughout the years. He has been constantly tagged as one of the very talented acts to watch out for in the coming years within the Ghanaian music circles.

His hard work, consistency, lyrical dexterity and delivery are among the numerous features that can’t be ignored. ‘Mad Thing’ is a great piece of music you will want to add to your playlist and play endlessly.
