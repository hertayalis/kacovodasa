Luke Hemmings net worth-Australian musician, Luke Robert Hemmings was born on July 16, 1996 in Freemans Reach, New South Wales in Australia.

The present lineup of 5 Seconds of Summer was formed when the trio eventually included their common friend Ashton Irwin in their videos. On December 3, 2011, they had their first official show in a tavern in their hometown.
