Kendall Jenner's Skeletal Bodycon Dress Is So See-Through

June 10, 2024
blog

Kendall Jenner has put her own spin on the naked dress trend.

Today, the model and 818 founder shared a video of herself posing in front of a mirror while wearing a white bodycon midi dress from Alaïa. The short-sleeved, mock turtleneck piece featured abstract finishes: an embroidered skeleton-esque structure protruding from the ruching details at the center, and sheer, see-through mesh panels between the skeletal embellishments.

The Alaïa dress is part of Jenner's KJ x FRWD collab.

Instagram

Lately, Jenner has been seen in a number of statement-making dresses.

While celebrating Easter with her family this past Sunday, Jenner pulled a '90s-era Chloé silk slipdress for the festivities. The slip came in a blueish silver color, and also featured thin halter straps and white lace trim all across the neckline. She accessorized sparingly, wearing nothing else but black rectangular sunglasses from DMY BY DMY.

And during a trip to Paris last month, Jenner stepped out in a cozy gray monochromatic outfit which included a gray wool dress by Ann Demeulemeester. The dress had long sleeves, an asymmetrical thigh-high slit, and a mock neck with an attached full-length scarf adornment over the right shoulder. She finished the look off with knee-high pointed-toe leather boots by The Row, a spacious black leather tote bag, and black rectangular sunglasses.

Jenner has been embracing fashion's renewed affinity for all things sheer, too. In March, she posted photos of herself lounging in bed while wearing a see-through, body-hugging tank minidress and knee-high leather boots.
