Jennifer Garner Wore 5 Incredible Spring Outfits in One Day

August 28, 2024
blog

When she's not on a red carpet, Jennifer Garner is most often spotted out and about with her kids wearing workout clothes, jeans, and comfortable staples. But this Tuesday, while promoting her Apple TV+ series The Last Thing He Told Me in New York City, the actress showed off her best business-chic style.

For an appearance on the Today Show, Garner wore a sophisticated springtime ensemble composed of a white collared shirt—which she rolled up at the sleeves—a baby pink leather pencil skirt, and glossy pumps in a rosy pastel shade. She accented the outfit with some golden jewelry, including layered chain necklaces, a delicate bracelet, and bar studs.

MediaPunch/Bauer-Griffin//Getty Images

She was later seen leaving the show in an elegant white suit made up of an oversized blazer and crisp tailored pants which were folded at the bottom. She styled the set with a rosy nude top and glossy square-toed maroon heels with a golden chain-like embellishment at the front. The actress accessorized simply with a diamond necklace and large silver hoop earrings.

Raymond Hall//Getty Images

For a talk on The Late Show With Stephen Colbert, the 13 Going on 30 star looked stunning in a bedazzled strapless silver midi dress, which she styled with glossy black pumps and silver jewelry.

MEGA//Getty Images

After the show appearance, she stepped out in a classy black-and-white outfit made up of a tweed long-sleeved minidress layered over a crisp white collared shirt, and worn with sheer black tights and black boot heels.

Gilbert Carrasquillo//Getty Images

The actress's fifth look for her busy day of engagements was a chic take on 9-to-5 style. She wore a long-sleeved top in a light brown hue paired with an ample dark brown skirt which was cinched at the waist with a warm-brown belt with a golden buckle. She finished the outfit off with the same maroon heels she wore before, dainty golden jewelry, and black sunglasses.

With all the looks, Garner wore her long bob in relaxed waves and had on subtle makeup and a rosy lip.

Gotham//Getty Images

The Last Thing He Told Me follows a family that is torn apart when one man disappears following a case of financial fraud at his place of employment. The Apple Original thriller series will be available to stream on Apple TV+ starting this Friday, April 14.
