Who is Michael Tylos former wife Hunter?

August 31, 2024
blog

MICHAEL Tylo was known as an American soap opera actor and most famously recognized for his role as Quinton Chamberlain on “Guiding Light.”

It was announced on September 30, 2021, that Tylo had passed away at the age of 72. Here's what we know about his relationship with second wife and actress Hunter Tylo.

Who is Michael Tylo’s ex wife Hunter?

Since 2010, Michael Tylo has been married to actress Rachelle Reichert, who is most famously known for her role in the 2012 movie Stealing Las Vegas.

Prior to his relationship with Rachelle, Tylo was married to American actress, author and former model Hunter Tylo.

Born July 3, 1962, Hunter, 59, was married to Tylo from 1987 to 2005.

Hunter is most famously known for her role as Dr Taylor Hayes on The Bold And The Beautiful, which she portrayed from 1990 to 2019.

She was joined on the show by her husband in 2000, playing Sherman Gale.

Hunter appeared in Days Of Our Lives as Marina Toscano from 1989 until 1990, as well as having parts in The Maharaja's Daughter and Burke's Law.

What was Michael Tylo's cause of death?

Tylo's passing was announced by the University of Nevada, Las Vegas, where he had served as a a film professor. 

Nancy Uscher, the Dean of the College of Fine Arts, said in a statement: “Michael Tylo was a beautiful and caring human being — he was so special.

“He was an exemplary friend, colleague, teacher, and artist.

"He had such a distinguished and rich career, but he was humble about his accomplishments.

"He loved his family dearly and lived a joyous life, but he left us way too soon.

"We will miss him greatly, but feel deep gratitude about all that Michael contributed to the College of Fine Arts, UNLV, and the world.”

Despite being confirmed, a cause was not revealed.

Did Hunter remarry?

Prior to her marriage with Tylo, Hunter was married to Tom Morehart from 1980 to 1984.

After her marriage with Tylo ended, she then remarried again to Gersson Archila.

The two were married from 2009 to 2018.

As of 2021, she is reportedly single.

Does Hunter Tylo have any children?

Hunter is the mother of four children.

During her first marriage to Morehart, Hunter welcomed her first child, Christopher, 41, on January 22, 1980.

According to his Twitter bio, Christopher is now an "Anthropologist, ethnobotanist, environmental social scientist, Latin Americanist, & educator at Arizona State University".

During Hunter and Tylo's marriage, she welcomed three more children, one boy and two girls.

Their eldest, Michael Jr arrived on April 24, 1988, but tragically died in 2007 at the age of 19, following an accidental drowning.

Hunter's manager later told Reuters that he had fallen into the pool after suffering a seizure.
