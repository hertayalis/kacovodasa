Valorant Agent 'Iso' to Debut with the release of Episode 7: Act 3

June 29, 2024
blog

Riot Games has confirmed that a new Duelist Agent, Iso, will be added to VALORANT in Episode 7: Act III. The episode and the new agent are scheduled for release on October 31, 2023.

Iso is not your ordinary agent! His background gives him unique abilities that set him apart from other duelists. Originating from China, Iso brings a rich history and a new style of play that could make him a favorite for many players.

Though Iso has not been officially released yet, his addition as a Duelist Agent from China is already stirring discussions among the player community. Details on his specific abilities are still under wraps, but introducing a new agent could offer fresh tactical elements and change the current game meta.
