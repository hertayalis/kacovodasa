Shanks Death is Almost Confirmed in Future Manga Chapters

July 11, 2024
blog

Shanks, the red-haired pirate whose charisma and strength have captivated fans since the inception of “One Piece”, has always been a beacon of mystery and power. His initial role, setting Luffy on a path towards becoming the Pirate King by entrusting him with the iconic Straw Hat and the Gum Gum Fruit, has been pivotal. His actions, whether intentional or serendipitous, have always seemed to steer the larger narrative towards a cataclysmic showdown in the pirate world.

The Peacekeeper’s New Path: Shanks’ Surprising Shift in the Final Chapters

In a startling turn of events, Shanks has abandoned his role as a peacekeeper, which he meticulously maintained by averting conflicts and even intervening in wars to prevent chaos in the pirate era. His meetings with influential figures like Whitebeard and the Five Elders always hinted at a deeper, overarching plan to maintain a fragile balance in the world. However, the Final Saga reveals a shift, showcasing a more aggressive and determined Shanks, who is now actively participating in the race to claim the One Piece.

In a conversation with Beckman, Shanks declares, “it is time for them to enter the great game and claim the One Piece for themselves.”

His aggressive stance towards claiming the One Piece, especially in the original Japanese context, signifies a drastic change in his character and intentions, potentially setting the stage for unprecedented conflicts.

The Perils Ahead: Shanks, The One Piece, and a Sea of Enemies

Despite being one of the strongest pirates, with a bounty that could arguably be the highest, especially after the fall of Kaido and Big Mom, Shanks’ decision to pursue the One Piece places him in the crosshairs of formidable enemies. His knowledge, possibly due to his connection with the Figarland Family, and his newfound goal make him a target for entities like the World Government and old rivals like Blackbeard (Marshall D. Teach).

A Looming Confrontation: Shanks, Blackbeard, and the Shadows of Death

Shanks and Blackbeard’s rivalry, marked by a tense history and the scars that Shanks bears, is inching towards a climactic confrontation. Blackbeard, despite not being perceived as a threat by some fans, embodies a lurking danger that has been gradually building throughout the series. His potential clash with Shanks could be a pivotal moment that defines the future of the pirate world and Luffy’s journey towards becoming the Pirate King.
Shanks has always been aware of the threat Blackbeard poses, and his actions and movements have often been in response to or in anticipation of Blackbeard’s schemes. The scars on Shanks‘ face, a constant reminder of Blackbeard’s menace, and their near-confrontation during the Paramount War, hint at an inevitable, possibly tragic clash between the two.

The Heart-Wrenching Possibility: Will Shanks Meet His Demise?

The death flags have been waving for Shanks since his first encounter with Blackbeard, and as the story propels into the Final Saga, the possibility of his demise becomes ever more plausible. Shanks, despite his formidable strength and strategic mind, might meet his end at the hands of Blackbeard, not necessarily due to a lack of power, but possibly to pave the way for Luffy to confront Blackbeard himself.
In a narrative where major players like the Kid Pirates and Heart Pirates have already faced crushing defeats, Shanks’ potential death could serve as a catalyst for Luffy’s final growth and ultimate showdown against Blackbeard. It could be a moment that transcends the typical shonen trope, providing a depth and gravity to the story that cements “One Piece” as a timeless epic.

Conclusion: A Tale of Pirates, Legacy, and the Unpredictable Sea

“One Piece”, available to read via Viz Media, and on platforms like Shonen Jump and Manga Plus, continues to weave a tale that balances epic battles, emotional depth, and the unpredictability of a world governed by pirates, marines, and the enigmatic seas. With Chapter 1094 set to release on October 8, 2023, fans are on the edge of their seats, awaiting the unfolding of Shanks’ fate and the ripple it will send across the oceanic world crafted by Eiichiro Oda.
