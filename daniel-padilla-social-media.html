Daniel Padilla Social Media: Daniel Padilla, also known as Daniel John Ford Padilla is a Filipino actor and recording artist.

He developed a passion for acting and singing at a younger age, has been consistent throughout his career, and has become one of the most sought-after acts.

Daniel Padilla Social Media

Daniel Padilla has impressive numbers on social media. He owns a verified X (Twitter) account with over 9.3 million followers, a Facebook page with over 1.6 million followers, and a verified Instagram account with over 6.3 million followers.

Padilla made his television debut with a supporting role in the soap opera Gimik 2010 (2010), before landing the lead role in Growing Up in 2011.

He went on to appear in the television series; ‘Princess and I’ (2012–2013), ‘Got to Believe’ (2013–2014), ‘Pangako Sa Yo’ (2015–2016), ‘La Luna Sangre’ (2017–2018), and ‘2 Good 2 Be True’ (2022).

In films, Padilla debuted as a supporting actor in 24/7 in Love and Sisterakas (both 2012). He later starred in the box office hit film; ‘Must Be… Love’, and Pagpag: Siyam na Buhay (both 2013).

He also starred in ‘She’s Dating the Gangster’ (2014), ‘Crazy Beautiful You’ (2015), ‘Barcelona: A Love Untold’ (2016), ‘Can’t Help Falling in Love’ (2017), and ‘The Hows of Us’ (2018).

Padilla’s films have earned a total of ₱3.14 billion at the box office. He’s recognized as one of the most prominent young actors in the Philippines.

As a recording artist, Padilla has released projects including; Daniel Padilla (2012), DJP (2013), I Heart You (2014), I Feel Good (2015), and the compilation album DJ’ Greatest Hits (2016).

He has also held solo concerts including Daniel Live! (2013), DOS (2014), Most Wanted (2015), and DJ Classics: Back at the Araneta (2017).

Padilla received multiple accolades across television, film, and music, including the FAMAS Award for Best Actor and PMPC Star Award for Movie Actor of the Year, as well as three World Music Awards nominations.
