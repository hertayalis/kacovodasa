Exploring the ESPN announcers for the Colorado-Colorado State game

Mark Jones

Jones is a Canadian sportscaster who has been a fixture in the world of sports broadcasting since 1990. He is a familiar voice in both college football and NBA games and is a seasoned member of ESPN's broadcasting family.

Louis Riddick

The former NFL safety's (1991–1998) football pedigree runs deep, with his brother, Robb Riddick, an RB for the Buffalo Bulls over eight seasons. Riddick is an analyst on ESPN's Monday Night Football.

Quint Kessenich

Kessenich is a seasoned American broadcaster, gracing ABC and ESPN screens since 1993. His versatile coverage spans lacrosse, basketball, football, hockey, wrestling, and horse riding.

What did the Colorado State coach say about Deion Sanders?

Colorado State coach Jay Norvell stirred the pot ahead of the matchup by commenting on Deion Sanders' coaching style.

“I don’t care if they hear it in Boulder, I told [the team] when I talk to grownups, I take my hat and glasses off. That’s what my mother taught me,” Norvell said on The Coach's show.

Norvell later attempted to play down his comments, saying they were misconstrued in the media.
