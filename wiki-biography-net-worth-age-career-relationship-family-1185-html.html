  • Hidrent is a platform offering trusted home services, employing off-duty firefighters for various projects.
  • Services provided by skilled professionals include Light Fixtures, General handymen, Furniture Moving, Holiday Lights, Smoke Detectors, Hanging, Hauling, Furniture Assembly, TV Wall Mount, and Gutter Cleaning.
  • Customers pay approximately $100 to $300 for each job.
  • Hidrent deducts 20% of the payment and gives 80% to the firefighter.

Who Is the Founder of Hidrent?

  • The founder of Hidrent is David Heimbuch, a professional salesperson who has worked for several companies including Tapad, AT&T Mobility, and Shazam Entertainment.
  • He studied Business Administration at the University of Nebraska-Lincoln and is an Accelerator Program member of Capital Factory.

What Happened After Shark Tank?

  • In Shark Tank, David Heimbuch asked for a $3.7 million valuation but signed a deal worth $937k with Lori and Robert for 33.3% equity.
  • Hidrent is now providing services nationwide, with potential expansion to cater to senior citizens and single mothers.
  • The company had a lifetime sale of $850,000 with revenues of $125,000 in 2019, $260,000 in 2020, and $325k in 2021, taking 23% of these sales.
  • Hidrent raised $662.5k from 9 investors in a seed round before Shark Tank.
  • The company also ran an equity-based fundraiser on StartEngine, raising $143,080.02 at an $8.5 million valuation.
  • A partnership with the International Association of Fire Fighters until 2024 will allow Hidrent to connect with 300,000+ firefighters.
  • As of December 2022, Hidrent’s annual revenue is approximately $1.4+ million.
