Biography

Pamela Rabe was born in Oakville, Canada on April 30, 1959. Known for her roles in such 1990s feature films as Paradise Road, Cosi, and Sirens, she also performed in numerous Australian theatrical productions. Her television credits include appearances in Wentworth and Holly’s Heroes. She co-starred with Cate Blanchett in a Sydney Theatre Company staging of The War of the Roses. She began her stage and screen career after training at the Playhouse Acting School in Vancouver, British Columbia, Canada.
