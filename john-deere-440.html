The John Deere 440 is the industrial version of the ohn Deere model 435. John Deere 440 Industrial tractor was available with rubber tires or in a tracked crawler version.

The gasoline engine is an updated version of the engine from the John Deere model 430, with higher compression and rated RPMs.

John Deere 440 Industrial Tractor featured a General Motors 1.7L 2-cyl diesel and John Deere 1.9L 2-cyl gasoline engine, 4×2 2WD, power assist steering, and 4085 to 4421 pounds weight.

John Deere 440 Industrial tractor attachments include John Deere 71 Loader front-end loader, John Deere 50 Backhoe, and John Deere 51 Backhoe.
