Jennifer Lopez Wore an All-Black Suit for Breakfast Date with Ben Affleck

July 28, 2024
blog

Jennifer Lopez's romantic date style has officially made its seasonal transition into fall.

Yesterday morning, the multi-hyphenate stepped out in Beverly Hills for a fashionable breakfast date with husband Ben Affleck.

For the occasion, Lopez opted for a head-to-toe black ensemble, consisting of a belted jacket with gold buttons and the collar popped up. She paired the chic outerwear with roomy, wide-leg trousers. She capped off the look with sky-high stiletto platform boots in black, a mini croc-embossed black Hermès Birkin bag, violet aviator sunglasses, and chunky gold hoop earrings.

Photos of Affleck show him in a more relaxed fall outfit. The Deep Water actor wore a camel-colored shearling jacket, a cream sweater, white trousers, suede dress shoes in beige, and black aviator sunglasses.

X17online.com

The couple recently threw a lavish, star-studded wedding ceremony in Riceboro, Georgia. The fete came after the two legally became husband and wife in a surprise Las Vegas wedding chapel ceremony.

The "Let's Get Loud" singer reflected on the big day in a special edition of her fan newsletter, On the JLo.

"Ben and I laughed the night before about getting married again at our age. We had both been married before and we aren't exactly kids any more but somehow now seemed like the only age that made sense," she wrote. "I had recently read something Rainer [Maria] Rilke wrote in 'Letters to a Young Poet' about love. He said one has to be ready for love. 'For one human to love another is the most difficult task. It is the work for which all other work is merely preparation.' Being able to love someone so that you want to be better for them and make them happy, because giving happiness and love becomes more joyful than receiving it, is true sublime adult love."
