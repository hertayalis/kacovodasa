UNIVERSAL CITY, CALIFORNIA: Ever since Blake Shelton's departure from 'The Voice,' fans have wondered about his next move.

The secret unfolded during 'The Voice' Season 24 Live, revealing that Blake will now be hosting the game show 'Barmageddon'.

In a surprise appearance during the Live Performance episode, Nikki Bella announced that she, alongside Blake and Carson Daly, will be hosting USA Network's new show.

Blake uploaded a slew of photos ahead of the release of 'Barmageddon' on USA. The former 'The Voice' coach was dressed up like Santa in the photos.

In the last slide, he was seen entertaining his audience while playing the drums.

Carson, on the other hand, was dressed in a green dress with a green hat but red pants.

Bringing on the sexy Christmas vibe, Nikki accompanied Blake as she sported a white tank top and leather pants.

Blake captioned the post, "Santa's trading in the egg nog tonight!!! Don't miss the #Barmageddon Holiday Special on @nbc after @nbcthevoice!"

What is 'Barmageddon' all about?

'Barmageddon' is a TV game show created and co-produced by Blake Shelton. In the show, Blake, along with co-hosts Carson Daly and Nikki Bella, invites celebrity guests to his bar in Nashville.

The audience, enjoying Ole Red's front-row seat, witnesses the celebrity guests competing in various over-the-top bar games.

Carson is in charge of behind-the-bar arrangements, while Blake takes the stage, singing music and entertaining the audience.

The bar games in 'Barmageddon' include activities like Keg Curling, Drunken Axe Hole, Sharts (Shelton Darts), and more.

The show combines elements of competition, entertainment, and humor in a bar setting, providing a unique and engaging experience for both participants and viewers.

'Barmageddon' premieres on December 5 at 11 pm EST on USA Network.
