Hilary Farr is back on our screens with another series of Love It or List it on, but how old is the HGTV host?

Love It or List is a television series that helps out homeowners who are looking to either sell or renovate their homes.

With the help of Hilary and David, they will turn their home into the house of their dreams, or move to a new property that suits them better.

Hilary Farr is a woman of many talents, we found out her age below!

Who is Hilary Farr?

Hilary Farr is a British-Canadian designer, television host, actress, and businesswoman.

She is best known for co-hosting Love It or List It on HGTV with actor and realtor David Visentin.

She has since founded Hilary Farr Designs, which offers all sorts of decorations for homes such as furniture, lighting, rugs, and accessories.

Hilary also has a new series, premiering on October 18th 2021, called Tough Love with Hilary Farr, where she will use her design expertise to help families who are struggling to renovate their homes.

How old is Hilary Farr?

Hilary Farr was born on August 31st, 1951, making her 70 years old.

Love It or List It premiered back in 2008, so she was 57 when she began hosting the show!

She was born in Toronto, Canada, however grew up in London, England, hence the British accent.

Her interest in interior design at a young age, whilst her mother let her help decorate her childhood bedroom.
