Clint Eastwood's divorce from second wife Dina is finalised

June 07, 2024
blog

    Clint Eastwood is officially divorced. The 84-year-old actor and Dina Eastwood have finalised their separation, according to court documents filed in Monterey, California on Monday. The pair married in 1996 and have one child together, daughter Morgan, 18. Their split was confirmed in August 2013, and by then the couple had already been living apart for some time. Dina then filed for legal separation that September.

    Clint Eastwood and Dina Eastwood have finalised their divorce

    Several sources said the marriage started to suffer when they family started filming the reality show, in 2012. Oscar-winner Clint, who has six other children, was only an occasional guest star. "Clint was furious about the show," a source told . "It went against everything he stands for; he's incredibly private, and she put his kids on TV… It was not a happy time for them." The couple seem to have remained on good terms since their split, however. Just a year ago, Dina called her ex "the sweetest guy" in an interview on TV chat show .

    Dina Eastwood with Morgan Eastwood (right) and Clint's daughter Francesca (centre)

    "I don't think we will be getting back together," the 49-year-old said. "That is why I filed for divorce. I think maybe a part of me was holding out, like, 'What are we doing here?' Then there have been some definite signs that we're not going to get back together, so let's move on amicably is my opinion…"

    Asked how Clint was handling the split, Dina added, "He's lovely, he hasn't done much. It's other people around him have done things that have blown me away, and again my future ex-husband has done nothing (wrong). "He's probably the sweetest guy I've ever met. He is the sweetest, he is a loving, kind, low-key person, so my intuition was still great on marrying a good person."
